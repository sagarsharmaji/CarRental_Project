PROCESS WORKFLOW




User has to Register to the system as a new user


1)Customer


• You can login as service customer to login you have to enter valid username and password. • After login you can select the required automobile service like breakdown, battery jumpstart, etc. • You can add location and service time details. • Now you have to confirm the booking. • Now you have to do online payment. • Vendor will be allotted to you and your request will be confirmed. • Now you can log out successfully.

2)Service Provider


• New user has to first register to the system giving details such as name, email, phone number, aadhar card number, PAN card number, etc. • You can login as vendor, to login you have to enter valid username and password. • After login he has to add the details such as list of services provided by him, his garage location, garage name, service description. • And now he as to confirm the details. • If any changes the can later login and do the editing. • Now he can now logout from the system.
